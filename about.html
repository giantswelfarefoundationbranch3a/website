About Us

First India - based International Service Organization Founded - 31st August 1972 It is our commitment that with Generosity in our heart and with Integrity as our guide, we shall carry out our Actions and with Nobility in our heart and Truth as our guide we shall do Service that we shall be GIANTS. Giants International Charitable Trust is registered and donations are exempted under 80G under IT. Over 630 groups existing as on December 2017.
